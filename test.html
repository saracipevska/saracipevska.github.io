Table tennis camps

Vlasina Kamp 2022

My first camp was in Vlasina, Serbia. It was one of the most efficient camps I've been a part of. There were coaches from 5 different countries sharing their knowledge, skills and advices with all of the players. It lasted 7 days and improved my shape and skills definitely. Would recommend to visit and be a part of Vlasina Kamp if you are a table tennis player and also if you want to make a lot of new great friends! All the fun we had was just a great bonus! Here are some of the pictures! Enjoy.

Krupanj Kamp 2022

I was part of the international table tennis camp in Krupanj, Serbia. We worked professionaly, with profesional coaches. The camp lasted 8 days and the practice time was 8 hours a day. There were included some of the best players and coaches of Serbia and Romania. I definitely learned most of my knowledge and skills there.
